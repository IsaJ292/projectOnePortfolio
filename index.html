Hi Welcome Home!



Some things about Isa is that she loves exploring the city and this picture in the top right corner is from a time when her and her roommates went to the beach at night to light sparklers and fireworks. Isa is originally from the Central Valley, from a small town called Waterford and left at the age of 18 for college and has since lived in SF. Currently majoring in Industrial Design, Isa enjoys the ability to be creative in the workplace compared to when she was a Computer Science Major. Unlike her hometown, city life is a big jump and is one that she really loves. A lot of people love to hate the city and its bad factors, but Isa loves the amount of opportunity the city provides people who look for it. There are endless possibilities, and she finds it kind of crazy how good public transportation is here contrary to Waterford. Other things about Isa is that she has a deep love for matcha and tends to make her own lattes at home since there are very few places that she finds are of good quality in the Bay Area. Isa has a fear of dogs, mainly bigger ones nowadays but has had many cats throughout her life. Currently she has two cats, one named Kitty who is 7 years old and one named Michi who is 5 months old. Her goal after graduation is to travel the world before settling down. She eventually wants to get an apartment in the city, get a Shiba Inu and a cat, and then ideally work as a Industrial Designer for Apple or at a smaller design firm. In the end she hopes she can become a meaningful designer that is able to provide for her immigrant parents who gave it there all to send her to college and to her older sister who supported her the whole time.
